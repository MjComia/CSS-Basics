Popular Websites

Youtube

YouTube is a video-sharing platform where users can upload, watch, like, share, and comment on videos. Launched in 2005 and now owned by Google, it is one of the largest websites globally, offering content ranging from entertainment and education to live streaming. It also provides monetization options for creators through ads, memberships, and sponsorships.

Facebook

Facebook is a social networking platform that allows users to connect with friends, family, and communities. Launched in 2004, it has grown to become one of the most popular websites globally, offering features such as profiles, news feeds, groups, events, and marketplace. It also owns other popular platforms like Instagram and WhatsApp.

Reddit

Reddit is a social news aggregation and discussion website where users can share content, vote on posts, and engage in discussions. Launched in 2005, it has grown to become one of the most visited websites globally, known for its diverse communities (subreddits) and user-generated content. It covers a wide range of topics, from news and entertainment to niche interests and hobbies.

Instagram

Instagram is a photo and video-sharing platform that allows users to post, like, comment, and share visual content. Launched in 2010 and now owned by Facebook, it has become one of the most popular social media websites globally, known for its focus on visual storytelling and engagement. It also offers features like stories, reels, and IGTV.

Github

GitHub is a platform for hosting and collaborating on code repositories. Launched in 2008, it has become one of the largest code-sharing websites globally, used by developers, organizations, and open-source projects. It offers features like version control, issue tracking, and pull requests, enabling users to work together on software development projects.
